Gardening

This is everything we plan to do in the garden. From layouts and seed catalogs to computers and software to help our garden grow more efficiently.

Plans and Layouts

As we plan our garden from season to season we will add our layouts and ideas for the garden. We may also include templates for creating your own layouts and tracking your seeds and plant growth.

SteadyHand

SteadyHand is our set of tools for running the garden. It will invole Raspberry Pi's running custom software to help manage and maintain the garden. This is a work in progress and more infromation will be added as we finalize plans.

Plants In Season

Plants In Season is our version of a digital almanac for the best planting times, best plants for each season, and maintenance tips for your zone.
