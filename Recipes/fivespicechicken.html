Five-Spice Crispy Chicken

Shatteringly crisp skin with warm, aromatic spices.

Ingredients

  • 4 bone-in, skin-on chicken thighs
  • 1 tsp Five-Spice powder
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 3 scallions (minced)
  • 1 tbsp minced ginger
  • 5 tbsp neutral oil

    • Instructions

    1. Score the chicken skin. Rub with spice mix(Five-Spice, salt and pepper). Let sit for 15 mins.
    2. Place chicken skin-side down in a cold pan. Turn heat to medium. Cook for 10-12 mins until skin is dark gold and crispy.
    3. Turn and cook for 5-8 mins until done.
    4. Heat 3 tbsp oil until shimmering; pour over minced scallions/ginger in a heat-proof bowl.
    5. Spoon the aromatic oil over the crispy skin.

    Five-Spice Chicken(Breast/Lean Version)

    A tender, silky alternative to the crispy thigh version.

    Ingredients

    • 2 large chicken breasts (skinless)
    • 1 tsp Five-Spice powder
    • 1/4 tsp black pepper
    • 1 tsp cornstarch
    • 3 scallions (minced)
    • 1 tbsp minced ginger
    • 5 tbsp neutral oil

      • Instructions

      1. Slice chicken into thick strips or medallions. Mix Five-Spice powder, salt, pepper, and cornstarch. Toss chicken in the spice/cornstarch mixture to coat evenly.
      2. Heat 2 tbsp oil in a pan over medium-high heat. Once hot, sear chicken for 3–4 minutes per side until cooked through.
      3. Heat 3 tbsp oil separately until shimmering; pour over minced scallions/ginger in a heat-proof bowl.
      4. Spoon the aromatic oil generously over the tender chicken pieces.

      Recipe Notes

      • I am not a fan of thighs and tend to make this with chicken breast most frequently. I feel that the breast absorbs the flavors more.
