Shrimp & Snow Pea Stir-Fry

A high-heat, crunchy meal that relies on ginger and citrus for flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 lb large shrimp
  • 2 cups snow peas (trimmed)
  • 2 tbsp freshly grated ginger
  • 2 tbsp sake (or rice wine)
  • 1 tsp honey
  • 1 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • juice of half a lemon

    • Instructions

    1. Mix the ginger, sake, honey, and lemon in a small bowl.
    2. Get a wok or large skillet very hot. Toss in the shrimp and cook until they just turn pink (about 90 seconds).
    3. Add the snow peas. Stir-fry for another 2 minutes—you want the peas to stay bright green and snappy.
    4. Pour in the ginger-citrus sauce. Let it bubble and coat everything for 30 seconds.
    5. Turn off the heat and drizzle with sesame oil. Serve immediately.

    Recipe Notes

    • We sometimes add bean sprouts or other veggies to this as well.
    • We have found that if you stir fry this with the rice it changes the flavor. We recommend to serve over jasmine rice.
